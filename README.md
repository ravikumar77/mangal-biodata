* **Flexible Dynamic Builder:** Easily add custom fields, edit section titles (like Personal Details, Family Details, Contact), and reorder them with simple up/down arrows.

## 🚀 How to Use

1. Go to the [Mangal Biodata App](https://ravikumar77.github.io/mangal-biodata/).
2. Fill your **Personal Details** (Name, DOB, Height, Gotra, Education, Occupation).
3. Update your **Family Details** (Father's Name, Mother's Name, Siblings).
4. Select your favorite **Template** from the left pane to instantly stylize your profile.
5. Once your biodata looks perfect in the live preview, click **Download PDF** or **Download Image**.
